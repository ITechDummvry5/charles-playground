How to add an icon to a product

1
Tap any icon card here — the filename is copied to clipboard automatically.
2
Go back to the tracker and tap + Add Product or the edit icon on a card.
3
In the Icon field, paste the filename (e.g. rice.png) — the preview appears instantly.
4
To add a new icon: put the .png file in your icons/ folder, then add the filename to iconLibrary in the JS.
