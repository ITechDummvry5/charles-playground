📌 Top Stats — What Each Number Means

Total Spent

Shows everything you have spent so far. Automatically increases every time you add a product.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Just add items — the total updates by itself.
Example: Add Rice ₱65 + Soap ₱30 = Total Spent ₱95

Budget Left

How much money you can still spend. Turns yellow when almost out, red and says "over" when exceeded.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Always check this before buying anything.
Shows "—" if no budget is set yet. Set your budget first!

Items

Total number of products you have added to the list this week.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Use this to avoid overbuying.
More items = higher total spending.

Checked Off

How many items you already picked up at the market. Tap ✓ on a product card to check it off while shopping.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap ✓ on a card as you put it in your cart.
Checked items turn gray with a green ✓ badge so you know what's done.

💰 Budget Control Panel

Weekly Budget Bar

A progress bar that fills up as you spend. Green = safe · Yellow = almost out · Red = over budget.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Set your budget first, then start adding products.
Example: Budget ₱500, Spent ₱400 → bar is 80% full and turns yellow.

Set Budget

Type your spending limit and tap "Set Budget" to start tracking. Budget is saved — it stays even if you refresh.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Set this once at the start of the week.
Example: Type 500 → click Set Budget → bar starts tracking.

+ Add Extra Budget

New

Adds more money on top of your existing budget without losing your spending history. Safer than resetting.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Use this when you need extra money mid-week.
Example: Budget ₱500, click "+ Add Extra" → type 200 → new budget becomes ₱700.

Reset Budget

Clears the budget limit only. Your products and total spent are NOT deleted.

⚠ WHAT TO DO:
Only use this when starting a new week or new budget cycle.
IMPORTANT: Items stay. Only the budget limit goes to zero.

📊 Spending Breakdown

🍚 Food

Shows how much of your budget went to food items like rice, ulam, baon, groceries, eggs, etc.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Select "Food" category when adding food products.
Bar updates automatically every time you add or delete a food item.

🧺 Home

Tracks spending on household items like detergent, cleaning supplies, dishwash, gas, etc.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Select "Home" category for anything used around the house.
Example: Detergent ₱80 → Home category.

🧴 Personal

Tracks personal care spending — soap, shampoo, lotion, gamot, and similar items.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Select "Personal" when adding health and hygiene items.
Example: Shampoo ₱55 → Personal category.

🧾 Bills

New

Tracks recurring bills like electricity, water, internet, rent, Lazada, Shopee orders.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Select "Bills" for any payment or subscription.
Example: Electricity ₱1,200 → Bills category.

📦 Other

Anything that doesn't fit the other categories goes here.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Use "Other" for miscellaneous spending.
Example: School supplies, gifts, unexpected expenses.

🛒 Product Cards — How to Use

+ Add Product

Opens a form where you fill in the product name, category, quantity, price, icon, and optional note.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Fill name + price at minimum. Category defaults to Food.
All products are saved automatically — refreshing the page will NOT lose your data.

Icon Search

New

Type part of the filename in the icon field — a dropdown appears showing matching icons from your icons/ folder.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Type "ric" → rice.png appears → tap it to select.
Your icons: rice.png · kape.png · agahan.png · miryenda.png · ulam.png · baon.png · bills.png · lazada.png · shopee.png · groceries.png · tubig.png · egg.png · fastfooddelivery.png · gamot.png · delata.png · gas.png · school.png · default.png · edit.png

✓ Check Off

Tap the green ✓ button on any card while you are at the market to mark it as picked up.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap ✓ as you put each item in your cart.
Checked items turn gray with a ✓ badge. The "Checked Off" stat at the top updates automatically.

Edit Product (eye/edit icon)

New

Each product card has a centered edit icon below the product image. Tap it to open the edit form pre-filled with that product's details.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap the edit icon → change what you need → tap "Update product".
You can change the name, price, quantity, category, icon, or note. The card updates instantly.

✕ Delete Product

Tap the red ✕ button (top right of card, appears on hover) to permanently remove that item.

⚠ WHAT TO DO:
Only delete if you are sure — this cannot be undone.
Total spent and budget bar update automatically after deletion.

Category Filter

Tap All · Food · Home · Personal · Bills · Other to show only items in that category.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Use filters at the market to quickly find the right items.
Filtering does not delete anything — just hides other categories temporarily.

🧾 Actions Panel

🧾 Receipt

Shows a full summary table of all your products — name, quantity, category, and subtotal — with today's date.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap Receipt to review everything before paying or printing.
Total is shown at the bottom of the receipt.

🖨️ Print

Prints only the receipt — the rest of the page is hidden during print so it comes out clean.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Use for paper records or to show someone your spending list.
Receipt prints in black and white, readable on paper.

Clear All

Removes ALL products from your list permanently. Budget is not affected.

⚠ WHAT TO DO:
Only use when starting a brand new week.
WARNING: This cannot be undone. All items will be gone.

🌙 Dark / Light Mode

🌙 Dark Mode

New

The default mode — black background with white text. Easier on the eyes at night.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap the 🌙 button in the top right of the nav bar.
Your preference is saved — it stays dark even after refresh.

☀️ Light Mode

New

White background with dark text — just like iPhone's default look. Great for daytime use.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Tap the ☀️ button in the top right of the nav bar.
Everything — cards, modal, budget bar, footer — switches smoothly.

Auto-Save Theme

New

Whatever mode you pick is remembered by the app. You don't need to switch every time you open it.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Switch once — it stays your chosen mode forever.
Saved in your browser's local storage. Works without internet.

💾 Auto-Save — Your Data is Always Safe

Refresh Safe

New

All your products, budget, and checked-off items are saved automatically. Refreshing or closing the tab will NOT lose your data.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Nothing — it saves by itself every time you make a change.
Data is stored in your browser's local storage — no internet needed.

What Gets Saved

Everything is saved automatically: all products · prices · quantities · categories · icons · check-off status · budget amount · theme preference.

✔ WHAT TO DO:
Just use the app normally — saving is automatic.
Only "Clear All" and "Reset Budget" remove saved data — so use those carefully.

When Data Is Deleted

Data is only removed when you manually: tap "Clear All" (removes all products) or "Reset Budget" (removes budget only).

⚠ WHAT TO DO:
Think twice before tapping Clear All or Reset Budget.
Clearing browser history or cache can also erase saved data.
